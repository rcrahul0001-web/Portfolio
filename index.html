Employee Performance & HR Analytics

~17% Attrition Rate
1,470 Dataset Rows
2 Pages Power BI Report

Project Overview

This Business Intelligence project focuses on employee attrition and performance metrics. It addresses standard retention problems by highlighting critical metrics and departments experiencing high turnover, using a synthetic corporate HR dataset.

Data Pipeline & Engineering

  • Exploratory Analysis: Completed initial EDA using Python (Pandas, Matplotlib, Seaborn) to analyze data structures and identify correlations.
  • Database Integration: Configured a pipeline using Python's SQLAlchemy library to write clean, normalized datasets directly into a MySQL database, replacing manual imports.
  • Calculations & DAX: Modeled relational tables inside Power BI Desktop, writing custom measures for attrition counts, average salary scales, and performance distributions.

Critical Analytics Insights

  • Overtime Attrition: Revealed that employees who work overtime have a significantly higher attrition coefficient compared to non-overtime peers.
  • Satisfaction vs. Attrition: Attrition is concentrated in specific entry-level role buckets with low job satisfaction scores (1-2), suggesting focused salary/welfare corrections.

Mumbai Retail Shop Closure Analysis

33.6% Closure Rate
50,000 Shops Sampled
6 Charts Interactive Excel

Project Overview

A massive spatial and statistical retail research project. By exploring a record of 50,000 retail shops in Mumbai across 30 geographic areas, the model determines which indicators (rent index, business density, customer ratings) dictate commercial success or closure.

Analytical Approach

  • Data Cleaning: Managed missing entries, segmented shops by 20 distinct retail categories, and bucketed shop rents.
  • Excel Dashboarding: Programmed dynamic pivot tables and index-match slicers inside Microsoft Excel. Designed 6 detailed charts tracking key business indexes.
  • Correlation Analysis: Measured standard survival correlations showing a negative trend between high surrounding competitors, low ratings, and store durability.

Key Insights

  • Top Predictors: Shop closure probabilities are heavily driven by high monthly rent pressures combined with lower Google ratings (under 3.8 stars).
  • Vulnerable Segments: Clothing stores exhibited the highest attrition index, with local closure ratios exceeding 62% in high-rent zones.

Amazon India Sales Reporting

₹78.59M Total Revenue
120K+ Orders Analyzed
14.2% Cancellation Rate

Project Overview

A multi-page Power BI dashboard providing deep business insights into Amazon India's transaction records from April to June 2022. It features separate pages for Sales performance, Product analysis, and Regional tracking.

Implementation Steps

  • Data Modeling: Combined 7 distinct CSV datasets in Power Query, building a clean star schema model.
  • DAX Calculations: Programmed 8 primary DAX measures including Average Order Value (AOV) calculated at ₹652, B2B versus B2C distribution ratios, and transaction cancellation indexes.
  • Spatial Drill-Down: Formulated geographical drill-downs allowing managers to trace orders across 48 Indian states and 7,000+ local cities using maps and tree visuals.

Business Outcomes

  • Operational Optimization: Identified specific region-product couples experiencing cancellation rates above 25%, guiding shipping and stock allocation changes.
  • Product Focus: Uncovered that despite lower sales volumes, B2B bulk orders generated double the AOV of standard B2C orders, highlighting a growth segment.
